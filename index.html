What is the Newperleague?

The Newperleague is a revolutionary three-sport fantasy league. Created by insane sports fans that decided regular fantasy sports were not enough. The league consists of 12 team owners that each manage three fantasy sport teams.

Our Story

From humble beginnings to conglomerate status

  • 2014

    Conception of the Superleague

    The idea for a 3-sport fantasy league is conceived in a small windowless room in Portland, Maine

  • 2015 - 2016

    The Revolution Begins

    Newperleague's predecessors - the "Superleague" and "Super-DUPER League" lauch as incubators for the current league. Much like in the ABA-NBA merger, the Newperleague was founded out of it's two predecessors members.

  • August 2017

    THE DRAFT

    The ultimate fantasy sports draft. Over the course of 2 weeks, team owners took turns selecting from a pool of Football, Basketball & Baseball players at the same time.

  • July 2018

    Inagural Summer Meetings & Supplemental Draft

    Team owners took trains, busses and planes to convene in Princeton, New Jersey for a weekend of contenst and merriment.

  • August 2019

    2nd Annual Summer Meetings & Supplemental Draft

    Team owners convene in East Jewett, New York for a debaucherous three-day weekend.

  • 2020

    COVID TIMES :(

    Could have been better, NGL fam. Due to the cancellation of "real actual sports", there were no results for the 2020 Season.

  • August 2021

    3rd Annual Summer Meetings & Supplemental Draft

    Back on track, team owners break free for an action-packed weekend of swimming, golf, lawn games and whiffle ball at Chateau Badmington in New London, New Hampshire.

  • September 2021

    World's Greatest Poprawiny

    Andy (not pictured, lol) ties the knot with Karolina in a wonderful Polish-style wedding. Team owners flocked to a picturesque barnhouse in Delhi, NY to celebrate Andy through the traditional means of pounding vodka and cutting rugs.

  • August 2022

    4th Annual Summer Meetings & Supplemental Draft

    The Newperleague welcomes new-guy Malcom with golf, games and exciting trades in Blakeslee, PA.
